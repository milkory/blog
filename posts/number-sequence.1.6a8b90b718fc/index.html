解答

当 n≥2n \geq 2n≥2 时，有

an+1=Sn+1−Snan+1=(4an−1)−(4an−1−1)an+1=4an−4an−1an+1−2an=2an−4an−1an+1−2an=2(an−2an−1)an+1−2anan−2an−1=2\begin{equation*} \begin{aligned} a_{n+1} &= S_{n+1}-S_n \\ a_{n+1} &= (4a_n-1)-(4a_{n-1}-1) \\ a_{n+1} &= 4a_n-4a_{n-1} \\ a_{n+1} - 2a_n &= 2a_n-4a_{n-1} \\ a_{n+1} - 2a_n &= 2(a_n-2a_{n-1}) \\ \frac{a_{n+1} - 2a_n}{a_n-2a_{n-1}} &= 2 \\ \end{aligned} \end{equation*}an+1​an+1​an+1​an+1​−2an​an+1​−2an​an​−2an−1​an+1​−2an​​​=Sn+1​−Sn​=(4an​−1)−(4an−1​−1)=4an​−4an−1​=2an​−4an−1​=2(an​−2an−1​)=2​​

an−2an−1=3⋅2n−1a_n-2a_{n-1} = 3\cdot2^{n-1}an​−2an−1​=3⋅2n−1

an2n−an−12n−1=32\frac{a_n}{2^n} - \frac{a_{n-1}}{2^{n-1}} = \frac{3}{2}2nan​​−2n−1an−1​​=23​

an2n=12+32(n−1)\frac{a_n}{2^n} = \frac{1}{2}+\frac{3}{2}(n-1)2nan​​=21​+23​(n−1)
